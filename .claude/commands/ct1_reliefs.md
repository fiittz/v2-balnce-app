# CT1 Reliefs, Allowances & Surcharges

Company-level reliefs and allowances for CT1 Corporation Tax return preparation.

---

## Capital Allowances

| Asset Category | Rate | Period |
|---------------|------|--------|
| Plant & Machinery | 12.5% straight-line | 8 years |
| Industrial Buildings | 4% straight-line | 25 years |
| Motor Vehicles | 12.5% (capped at €24,000) | 8 years |
| Energy-Efficient Equipment (ACA) | 100% first year | 1 year |

**Balancing allowance/charge** on disposal — difference between tax written-down value and sale proceeds.
**Ref:** TCA 1997 Part 9

---

## Trading Losses (Companies)

| Relief | Description | Reference |
|--------|-------------|-----------|
| Carry forward | Against future trading income of the same trade | s.396A |
| Group relief | Surrender losses to group companies | s.411 |
| Carry back | 1 year against same trade profits | s.396B |

**Ref:** TCA 1997 ss.396A–411

---

## Close Company Surcharge

| Income Type | Surcharge Rate |
|-------------|---------------|
| Investment and estate income (undistributed) | 20% |
| Professional services income (undistributed) | 15% |

**Applies to:** Close companies (controlled by 5 or fewer participators) that do not distribute sufficient income.
**Ref:** TCA 1997 s.440

## Record Retention

All books, records, and linking documents must be retained for **6 years** from the end of the accounting period (TCA 1997 s.886).
