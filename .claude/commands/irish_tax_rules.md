# Irish Tax Rules — Quick Reference

Rates card and key deadlines. For detailed workflows and reliefs, see the commands listed at the bottom.

---

## Corporation Tax

| Type | Rate |
|------|------|
| Trading income | 12.5% |
| Non-trading income | 25% |

## Capital Gains Tax

- **Rate:** 33%

## Income Tax (2025/2026 — unchanged)

| Band | Rate | Single | Married (one income) |
|------|------|--------|---------------------|
| Standard | 20% | First €44,000 | First €53,000 |
| Higher | 40% | Balance | Balance |

## Tax Credits (2025/2026 — unchanged)

| Credit | Amount |
|--------|--------|
| Single Person | €2,000 |
| Married / Civil Partner | €4,000 |
| PAYE Credit | €2,000 |
| Earned Income Credit | €2,000 |
| Home Carer | €1,800 |
| Single Person Child Carer | €1,750 |

## USC

| Band | 2025 | 2026 |
|------|------|------|
| First €12,012 | 0.5% | 0.5% |
| Next band (2%) | €12,013–€27,382 | €12,013–€28,700 |
| 3% | to €70,044 | to €70,044 |
| 8% | €70,045–€100,000 | €70,045–€100,000 |
| 11% (self-employed surcharge) | €100,001+ | €100,001+ |

**Exempt** if total income < €13,000.

## PRSI Class S (Self-Employed)

| Period | Rate |
|--------|------|
| 2025 Jan–Sep | 4.1% |
| 2025 Oct–Dec / 2026 Jan–Sep | 4.2% |
| 2026 Oct+ | 4.35% |

Minimum contribution: €500/year. Threshold: €5,000.

## Key Deadlines

| Return | Deadline |
|--------|----------|
| CT1 | 9 months after accounting period end (23rd of the month, or 21st for large companies) |
| Form 11 | 31 October (mid-November via ROS) |
| Preliminary Tax | Same as Form 11 deadline |
