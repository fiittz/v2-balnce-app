# Irish Tax Rules — Quick Reference

Rates card and key deadlines. For detailed workflows and reliefs, see the commands listed at the bottom.

---

## Corporation Tax

| Type | Rate |
|------|------|
| Trading income | 12.5% |
| Non-trading income | 25% |

## Capital Gains Tax

- **Rate:** 33%

## Income Tax (2025)

| Band | Rate | Single Person |
|------|------|---------------|
| Standard | 20% | First €44,000 |
| Higher | 40% | Balance |

## USC (2025)

| Band | Rate |
|------|------|
| €0–€12,012 | 0.5% |
| €12,013–€27,382 | 2% |
| €27,383–€70,044 | 3% |
| €70,045–€100,000 | 8% |
| €100,001+ (self-assessed) | 11% |

**Exempt** if total income < €13,000.

## PRSI Class S

- **Rate:** 4.1% (min €500/year)

## Key Deadlines

| Return | Deadline |
|--------|----------|
| CT1 | 9 months after accounting period end (23rd of the month, or 21st for large companies) |
| Form 11 | 31 October (mid-November via ROS) |
| Preliminary Tax | Same as Form 11 deadline |
