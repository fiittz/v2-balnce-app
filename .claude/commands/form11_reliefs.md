# Form 11 Reliefs, Credits & Allowances

Individual-level reliefs and credits for Form 11/12 Income Tax return preparation.

## Medical Expenses Relief

- **Rate:** 20% of qualifying unreimbursed medical expenses
- **Qualifying expenses:** Doctor/consultant fees, prescribed drugs, hospital charges, physiotherapy, dental (non-routine), nursing home fees
- **Non-qualifying:** Routine dental, routine optical
- **Claim:** Form 11 (self-assessed) or Form Med 1
- **Ref:** TCA 1997 s.469

---

## Pension Contribution Relief

Relief at marginal rate on contributions up to age-based percentage of net relevant earnings:

| Age | Max % of Net Relevant Earnings |
|-----|-------------------------------|
| Under 30 | 15% |
| 30–39 | 20% |
| 40–49 | 25% |
| 50–54 | 30% |
| 55–59 | 35% |
| 60+ | 40% |

**Earnings cap:** €115,000 (2025).
**Ref:** TCA 1997 s.787

---

## Remote Working Relief

- **Rate:** 30% of vouched electricity, heating, and broadband costs for days worked from home
- **Claim:** Apportioned by days worked at home vs total working days
- **Employer contribution:** Up to €3.20/day tax-free (e-working allowance)
- **Ref:** TCA 1997 s.114A (as amended by Finance Act 2021)

---

## Flat-Rate Expenses

- Deduction by trade/profession at amounts published by Revenue
- Carpenter/joiner: check Revenue's flat-rate expense list
- No receipts required — fixed deduction from employment income
- **Ref:** Revenue list at revenue.ie

---

## Rent Tax Credit

- **Amount:** €750 per person / €1,500 per couple (2025)
- **Qualifying:** Rent paid on principal private residence, approved tenancy
- **Ref:** TCA 1997 s.473B (introduced Finance Act 2022)

---

## Charitable Donations Relief

- **Minimum donation:** €250 per year to approved body
- **Relief:** At marginal rate (paid to the charity, not as a credit to the donor — for PAYE taxpayers; self-assessed claim the deduction directly)
- **Max relief:** Based on 10% of total income
- **Ref:** TCA 1997 s.848A

---

## Small Benefit Exemption

- **Limit:** €1,000 per year (up to 2 vouchers/benefits)
- **Conditions:** Non-cash benefit (voucher, gift card), not convertible to cash
- **Ref:** TCA 1997 s.112B

---

## Capital Allowances (Sole Traders)

| Asset Category | Rate | Period |
|---------------|------|--------|
| Plant & Machinery | 12.5% straight-line | 8 years |
| Industrial Buildings | 4% straight-line | 25 years |
| Motor Vehicles | 12.5% (capped at €24,000) | 8 years |
| Energy-Efficient Equipment (ACA) | 100% first year | 1 year |

**Balancing allowance/charge** on disposal — difference between tax written-down value and sale proceeds.
**Ref:** TCA 1997 Part 9

---

## Trading Losses (Individuals — Sole Traders)

| Relief | Description | Reference |
|--------|-------------|-----------|
| Carry forward | Against future profits of the same trade | s.382 |
| Sideways relief | Against other income of the same year (restricted for non-active traders) | s.381 |
| Carry back | 1 year against same trade profits | s.382 |
| Terminal loss relief | Final 12 months of trade, carry back 3 years | s.385 |

**Ref:** TCA 1997 ss.381–385

## Record Retention

All books, records, and linking documents must be retained for **6 years** from the end of the accounting period (TCA 1997 s.886).
