01

The Real Concerns

1.

ROS Terms of Service

Automating a government portal could violate their ToS. Revenue hasn't approved bot-driven submissions.

2.

Credential Handling

An AI agent would need access to the digital certificate (.p12). That's the keys to someone's entire tax life.

01

The Real Concerns

3.

Liability

If the agent fills a wrong number and it gets submitted, who's responsible? The accountant? The software? The AI?

4.

GDPR / Data Protection

Tax data is highly sensitive. Routing it through an AI agent adds another data processor to the chain.

02

Where We Stand

Would we ship this tomorrow? No.

There are real security and compliance questions — credential handling, Revenue's ToS, liability, GDPR.

But the hard part is already done. The tax computation engine is live and accurate. Irish VAT rules, CT1 add-backs, RCT credits, capital allowances — all automated from a single bank CSV.
02

Where We Stand

The form-filling is the last mile.

The regulatory framework will catch up.

This is the direction we're heading. The data computation is live and accurate today. The autonomous filing is a capability demo — showing what becomes possible when the regulatory wrapper is in place.
